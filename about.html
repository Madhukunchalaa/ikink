About Us

About us

iLink Insurance Brokers are a young and proactive team of General Insurance brokers. At iLink Insurance Brokers we are dedicated to providing our clients with expert and innovative advice to service their precise insurance needs

We offer a wide range of General Insurance products and Risk Management solutions to clients. We know and study the General Insurance marketplace and its trends.

At iLink Insurance Brokers, our clients are our number one priority and we place their interests first. We are dedicated to provide an ‘end to end’ insurance service to our clients by identifying and analysing the risk exposure of our clients and/or their business, to tailor insurance solutions accordingly.

We provide a holistic service to all clients. An initial business planning session is essential to gain an insight into our client’s business or personal requirements, followed by negotiations with insurers for the most appropriate insurance cover to best represent their needs.

We work for you as your appointed agent to represent your interests first and foremost with your insurance arrangements, not the insurance company. Protecting your interests is our prime concern and we take personal care to structure your insurance cover to align with your insurance needs and budget. We have access to over 100 of Australia’s leading insurers and underwriting agencies. As your professional broker, we represent you (the client), not the insurance company – to ensure that you get the best available products and services at the best available price to suit your insurance requirements.

We don’t claim to be big, but our goal is to exceed our client’s expectations by offering tailored products with broad policy coverage at competitive price, and excellent professional service including claims services. We strongly believe that our role is not merely to sell insurances but to provide holistic services and advice to our clients to assist in managing risk exposure and ensuring peace of mind.

We provide advice and assistance to make sure you are properly protected against any unexpected losses.

Why Use An Insurance Broker

Insurance brokers are very different to insurance agents. As a broker, we act on behalf of you ‘the client’ to obtain the most appropriate and best value insurance for you and your needs.

Through our membership of leading peak bodies, we have a close connection with the national insurance market. These memberships ensure we receive regular updates on industry trends, current benefits, exclusions and costs of competing policies, which means our clients are privy to all the information relevant to their options – upfront.
